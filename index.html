Wrapless Lentil Wrap with Lime Joghurt Sauce

28/02/2021

Recipes I used:

    • Vegan Lentil Wrap
      Yoghurt sauce

    • We had a couple of leftover wraps that I made yesterday. They were a bit hard but still tasted good!
    I didn't feel like making more (and it was healthier without them) so I prepared just the contents of the wraps!

    Changes I made:

    The lentils called for lentils, turmeric, coriander, cumin red pepper flakes, tomato paste, garlic, and olive oil.
    I didn't use coriander, cumin, or red pepper flakes.
    I used about a cup of green lentils and a cup of red lentils (that's what I had!)

    I changed the salad quite a bit; I basically made a pico de gallo with added feta and without the coriander (my boyfriend isn't a massive fan!).
    So, I just used three tomatoes, half a cucumber, a red onion, the juice of half a lime, half a block of feta, and a big sprinkle of salt.

    I chose to make the yoghurt sauce from the ones listed on the recioe site.
    The original recipe called for lemon juice, I used the juice of half a lime.
    Instead of the parsley (which I didn't have), I used about a tablespoon and a half of dried dill.

    Next time...

    Use only green lentils.
